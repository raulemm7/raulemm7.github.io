ABOUT

My name is Raul-Emmanuel Oanea, and I am a young enthusiast passionate about programming and creating something beautiful and useful for people using the knowledge I've acquired in the field of computer science over time. I graduated from one of the most prestigious high schools in the country, "Stefan cel Mare" National College in Suceava, and I am currently pursuing my university studies at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at Babes-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca, with a major in computer science.

In terms of experience, I am one of the four founders of the game and application development club at the high school where I studied. Within this club, I serve as the main web designer and am responsible for the 2D and 3D graphics department. Additionally, whenever my schedule allows, I visit my former high school to share the knowledge I've gained from programming and application development competitions with the current club members. I also provide them with advice to aid in their application development process.

I am a sociable individual who enjoys working in teams and performs well under pressure. I thrive on meeting new people, especially those who share similar passions as mine, and I find joy in sharing information with them. Every day, I strive to evolve and learn as much as I can, constantly seeking new knowledge and opportunities for growth.
